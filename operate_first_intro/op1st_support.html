Getting started with Operate First#

We invite you to use the Operate First community as your practice space and incubator to get started operating first.

  • Website: https://www.operate-first.cloud/


  • GitHub Support Repository: https://github.com/operate-first/support

    • Start by reading a bit in the Community Handbook.

    • For questions, concerns, and requests, use an issue template or open a general issue.


  • Community and support chat: Join the community slack channel where we post announcements, general information, and more. If you have any questions, post a message to the #support channel.
